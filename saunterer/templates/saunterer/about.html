About Us

A mission to make people experience the city

With a mission to make people experience the city the way we see it, The Saunterers came into existence. We're a group of university students encouraged to change the perspective of India by providing more insights into this wonderful country, starting from the centre of the country, New Delhi. Being passionate travellers ourselves, we firmly believe that walking is the best way to discover a new place. We have walked the trails ourselves to bring out the best way to make you immerse yourselves into the local culture, traditions, food and history of the destination. Keeping in mind the diverse needs and interests of the people, we have options of customized walking tours so that you can explore what interests you the most while being on a budget. How we are different? We're a team of passionate students who are motivated to provide travellers with a truly local experience while being on a budget. We customize our tours according to your interests. Each of our tours has fixed itineraries but no script so that we can indulge you into the conversations with things you're passionate about while discovering the city on foot.
